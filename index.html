About me

Hi! I am a final year PhD student in Computer Vision at Ecole Normale Superieure, INRIA and Valeo.ai. My research focuses on learning problems in images that require less supervision including object discovery, self-supervised feature learning, weakly supervised object detection/localization and active learning.
